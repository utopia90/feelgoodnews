About Us

Good News, Our Passion


Feel Good News is your positive newspaper about the good things that are happening. When much of the media is full of doom and gloom, instead we are one of the first media organisationss in the world that is dedicated to quality, independent reporting about what’s going right. We are pioneers of ‘constructive journalism’ – a new approach in the media, which is about rigorous and relevant journalism that is focused on progress, possibility, and solutions. We publish daily online. As a magazine and a movement, we are changing the news for good.
